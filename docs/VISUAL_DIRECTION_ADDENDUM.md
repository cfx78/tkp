# Visual Direction Addendum

This implementation addendum refines `UI_AESTHETIC_BRIEF.md` without changing `MASTER_DOC.md` behavior, routes, content hierarchy, security, playback, or navigation.

## Screen formula

Every major screen combines a near-black foundation, one editorial focal point, controlled artwork or environmental illumination, and small precise interface detail. At rest, roughly 80–90% remains near-black, midnight navy, white, gray, or silver. Color behaves as concentrated light, never a full-screen wash.

Mobile is canonical. Preserve the compact branded header, generous dark space, thin dividers, selective square artwork, persistent bottom navigation, and one restrained active accent. Desktop expands that composition; it does not become a dashboard.

## Surfaces and composition

Prefer open dark space, direct text placement, thin dividers, square artwork, and occasional outlined interactions. Avoid uniform card grids, routine rounded containers, glass panels, large translucent blurs, and pills for ordinary labels. Music and selected content remain the focal light source.

## Identity and type

The approved mask is the primary symbol, never a mascot. The primary wordmark is heavy condensed uppercase with a crisp white center, restrained amber light to the left, and restrained cyan light to the right. Reserve it for the site name and rare identity statements.

Secondary display type is thin geometric uppercase with wide tracking. It may name `PERSONAL ARCHIVE`, Current Phase, Fixations, Listening Mode, lanes, section openings, or rare editorial labels. Optional directional extrusion is subtle and opt-in. Navigation, controls, metadata, body copy, and ordinary page headings remain functional typography.

## Illumination and environment

Later convergence may use rain-lit high-rises, metropolitan night, window glass, distant apartment light, restrained signage, implied room/listening objects, and artwork-derived cyan, magenta, gold, or coral light. Environmental artwork is not part of this identity task.

## Success test

The foundation must feel authored without environmental art, predominantly dark, melancholic, private, and calm; make TKP typography recognizable; keep music central; use fewer containers than a streaming dashboard; keep mobile definitive; and make the Kitsune memorable without character behavior. Aggressive futuristic, esports, generic cyberpunk, synthwave, and large blurry neon treatments fail.
